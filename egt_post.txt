Early gym gains quantified

As a new player, I quickly fell in love with Torn. Since then, I've spent a lot of time reading guides, forum posts, and community discussions. One of the things I enjoy most is understanding game mechanics and making decisions through data, simulations, and optimization.

In this post, I want to summarize what I've learned about early-game gym training, particularly during the period when players are still unlocking gyms. Rather than simply listing recommendations, I'll present the data behind each one and quantify its impact.

The gym gain formula used in these simulations is based on Vladar's reverse engineering, while the remaining data (gym unlock requirements, gym multipliers, etc.) comes from the Torn Wiki.

For each recommendation, I simulate two otherwise identical players:

One who follows the recommendation.
One who does not.

By comparing their progression, we can isolate and quantify the benefit of each decision.

The recommendations analyzed are:

Join a faction.
Get access to a Private Island.
Choose a training strategy (highest multiplier vs. balanced).
Decide whether working at a 3★ Music Store is worth the opportunity cost.
Happy Jumps and energy stacking.

Some recommendations are straightforward. Once you can afford a Private Island or join a good faction, the decision is usually easy.

Others involve genuine trade-offs.
